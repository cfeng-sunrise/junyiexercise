SOLUTION

  • \dfrac{N2}{\$C} = \dfrac{x}{N1}
  • \dfrac{N2}{N1} = \dfrac{\$C}{x}
  • \dfrac{N1}{N2} = \dfrac{x}{\$C}
  • \dfrac{x}{N2} = \dfrac{N1}{\$C}
  • \dfrac{N2}{x} = \dfrac{\$C}{N1}
